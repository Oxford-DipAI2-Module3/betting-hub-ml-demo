📍 Position Analysis - The Power Law

Critical Finding: Position exhibits extreme power law distribution. The gap between position 1 and position 10 is massive - approximately 10x difference in clicks.

Click Distribution by Position

Top 10 Positions

  • Position 1-3: 40% of all clicks
  • Position 4-6: 20% of all clicks
  • Position 7-10: 15% of all clicks
  • Position 11-20: 18% of all clicks
  • Position 21-30: 7% of all clicks

Key Insights

  • 75% of clicks happen in top 10 positions
  • Position 1 gets ~12-15x more clicks than position 30
  • Each position drop = ~8-12% fewer clicks
  • Premium bookmakers at position 15 underperforming
📊 Chart: Click Rate by Position (Exponential Decay)

Shows dramatic drop-off after position 3
Implication: Moving a high-value tile from position 15 to position 5 could increase its clicks by 3-4x. This is the core value proposition of ML ranking.

👤 User Behavior Insights

User Segmentation

🆕 New Users (0-2 sessions) - 45%

  • Click rate: 0.8 tiles per session
  • Prefer: Dabble, Sky Bet (familiar brands)
  • Avoid: Complex accas, high odds
  • Strategy: Show popular, simple bets

👤 Casual Users (3-20 sessions) - 38%

  • Click rate: 2.1 tiles per session
  • Prefer: Accas, specific bookmaker
  • Emerging patterns detectable
  • Strategy: Light personalization

⚡ Power Users (50+ sessions) - 17%

  • Click rate: 4.7 tiles per session
  • Drive 52% of total clicks (!)
  • Strong preferences: Mega Accas, boosts
  • Strategy: Heavy personalization

📊 Overall Distribution

  • Median: 3 sessions per user
  • Top 10% users = 60% of clicks
  • Top 1% users = 28% of clicks
  • Retention opportunity: Convert casual → power
Key Finding: Power users (17% of base) drive 52% of clicks. Personalizing for them has outsized impact on overall metrics.

💡 Key Takeaways

What We Learned

  1. Position dominates - 54% of model importance
  2. Quality variance is huge - 214x between best/worst tiles
  3. Boost tiles work - 2-3x better performance
  4. Power users matter - 17% of users = 52% of clicks
  5. Betway/BetMGM underpositioned - Major opportunity
  6. NCO waste is real - 40-60% shown to wrong users
  7. Personalization has impact - 3x lift when matched

What This Means for ML

  1. ML can reorder tiles to match user preferences
  2. Moving high-quality tiles up 5-10 positions = 3-4x more clicks
  3. Demoting NCOs for existing customers frees premium spots
  4. Power user personalization has outsized impact
  5. Expected improvement: +25-35% CTR
  6. With full personalization: +35-45% CTR
  7. Low-hanging fruit: Fix Betway/BetMGM positioning
